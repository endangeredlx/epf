## GRANT WRITING TIPS

**For EPF specifically:**
- Lead with impact numbers (100 moms served, etc.)
- Emphasize Detroit-specific focus
- Highlight volunteer engagement
- Show sustainability plan (recurring events)
- Include photos from events
- Get testimonials from recipients

- [ ] 501(c)(3) determination letter
