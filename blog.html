Our Programs

These programs were born from real needs I've seen in our community. Each one is designed to lift families up.

Programs

Building Stronger Families Through Life Skills

I've learned that sometimes we just need the right tools. Our workshops teach budgeting, stress management, parenting strategies, and conflict resolution. When parents feel confident, their whole family thrives.

Learn More
Programs

From Dream to Business

I believe every person has a gift. Our entrepreneur program helps turn those gifts into businesses. We walk alongside aspiring owners with business planning, micro lending support, and ongoing mentorship. Your dream matters.

Learn More
Programs

Growing Food, Growing Community

There's something powerful about putting your hands in the soil and growing your own food. Our agriculture program teaches urban farming, food preservation, and nutrition. Fresh produce for families, grown by families. It brings us together.

Learn More
Programs

Connecting Families to What They Need

Sometimes the hardest part is knowing where to start. Our Resource Access Hub connects families to housing assistance, childcare referrals, healthcare navigation, and employment support. You don't have to figure it out alone. We're here to help.

Learn More
Programs

The Duplex Initiative: A Place to Call Home

Every family deserves a safe, stable place to live. Through our partnership with Bringing Back the Block, we're creating affordable housing options right here in Detroit. A home changes everything for a family. I've seen it transform lives.

Learn More
Programs

Careers in Entertainment

This program is close to my heart. We partner with industry professionals to teach lighting, audio, and event production. These are real careers with real wages. Our young people deserve pathways to success in the creative industries.
