## 📋 Wish List System

### Concept:
Instead of generic baskets, personalize based on recipient wishes.

### Implementation:
- Add to intake form on website
- **5 things NEEDED** + **5 things WANTED**
- Frame as "fill out wish list for personalized basket"
- Can reuse for: Christmas, Thanksgiving, backpacks, winter warmup

### Process:
1. Partner orgs share link with residents
2. Recipients fill out wish list online
3. EPF organizes items by person/location
4. Assembly team matches items to individual baskets

## 💡 Key Lessons from Call

1. "Look like you got the money to get the money"
2. Frame intake as "wish list" not "application"
3. Takes 7-14 impressions for someone to donate
4. Pay to play on social media ($5-20 makes huge difference)
5. Street marketing still works
6. Copy corporate strategies (J&J event insights)
7. Diversity in photos/recipients builds trust
8. No Cash App/Zelle on website (scam risk)
9. Checks and balances - Tina approves all changes
10. Real designers > AI flyers (shows investment)
