# EPF UGC Campaign Kit
**Campaign:** "Share Your Story" Single Mom Giveaway
**Prize:** $50 Amazon Gift Card + Mother's Day Blessing Basket
**Deadline:** April 25, 2026

---

## CAMPAIGN OVERVIEW

Encourage single moms to share their stories on social media for a chance to win a $50 Amazon gift card AND a Blessing Basket. User-generated content builds community, identifies recipients, and creates authentic promotional material.

---

## ENTRY REQUIREMENTS

1. **Post** a photo or video on Instagram or Facebook
2. **Tag** @empowermentpathfoundation
3. **Use hashtag** #EPFMomStory
4. **Caption** must include: What being a mom means to you

**Eligibility:**
- Must be a single mother in the Detroit metro area
- Must follow @empowermentpathfoundation
- One entry per person
- Public account required for entry to be seen

---

- $50 Amazon gift card

## SELECTION CRITERIA

Judges will select winners based on:
1. **Authenticity** - Genuine, heartfelt story
2. **Impact** - Demonstrates resilience or community spirit
3. **Engagement** - Likes, comments, shares (secondary factor)
4. **Need** - Priority to moms facing challenges
